Sample Insights

Women are more likely to buy compared to men (~65%)

Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh are the top 3 states (~35%)

Adult age group (30–49 years) is the maximum contributing segment (~50%)

Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra channels are the highest contributing platforms (~80%)

Final Conclusion to Improve Vrinda Store Sales:

Target women customers in the 30–49 years age group living in Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh by promoting ads, offers, and coupons on Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra.
